The best tuna pizza

For the best tuna pizza you need a can of tuna, one onion, a lot of dough, some tomato sauce and a lot of cheese.

Bake it for 20 minutes in the oven until the crust of the dough is golden.

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • onion

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough
  2. Put your toppings on the pizza. Start with the sauce.
  3. Bake it, wait 20 min and enjoy!
